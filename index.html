Introduction

Welcome to the Tennis Typing Test, a framework designed to analyze and categorize the countless styles of play in tennis. Much like personality tests, this quiz places your game on four independent axes. Your results will give you a unique 4-letter acronym that captures your strategic approach, court positioning, point tempo, and technical foundation.

This isn't about finding a "best" style—it's about understanding your own. Whether you're an aggressive baseliner, a crafty constructor, or a flawless all-courter, your type has its own strengths and legendary players to learn from.
